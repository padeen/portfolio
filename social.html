Social with Chinese

Live streaming

Direct contact with the audience from mainland China through live streaming on the PandaTV network. Together with my friend Shao Zhang we are making shows about everyday life in the Netherlands. The audience can have a conversation with us during the recording. We only speak Chinese 100%.

Me and my friend Shao Zhang during a live streaming session in the city Rotterdam.

Chinese Food Culture

One of the most delightfull aspects I have encountered while learning about China is it's food culture. No matter the occasion, whether it's discussing some business or visiting a friend's house, usually there will be food involved.

My friend Xiao enjoying a hot bowl of Chinese noodle soup.

Helping people to learn a new language

I love to help out other people. During my life in the Netherlands I have found the opportunity to help other people in learning the Dutch language. At the same time all the people I have met are eager to introduce me to their own culture. Through this way I have learned a lot about foreign cultures and found some of the most valuable friendships in my life.

Me together with my friend Bobby Ding and his family. After studying Dutch Bobby Ding has passed all of his exams and he is now using the language be an active member of the Dutch society.
