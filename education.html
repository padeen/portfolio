Future perspective

I'm further increasing my value as a softwaredeveloper by gaining more experience with webframeworks. Aspects of webframeworks I'm particularly passionate about are: Test Driven Development, the Model-View-Controller pattern and the DRY principle. As of this writing the framework of my choice is Ruby On Rails. With my background in java and javascript the Ruby programming language feels like the perfect next step to challenge myself and thereby further develop my programming skills.

It is my final intention to take all the skills I learn during my time with the Ruby On Rails framework with me into the future and apply them to whatever challenges I will encounter in my career as a software developer.
