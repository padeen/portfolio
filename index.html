Passion for technology and other cultures

Learning new things is what I like doing most in life. Information Technology and learning about other cultures is what excites me the most.

During my study of Information Technology in the international city Rotterdam I became passionate to learn about other cultures. I had found the opportunity to help other people in learning the Dutch language. After my study of Information Technology I have studied Japanese and Chinese for three years to learn more about language and culture.
